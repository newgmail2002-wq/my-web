Questions

AI Ads FAQ

Will it look fake?
Not if it's done properly. Bad AI ads look fake because someone typed a prompt and shipped the first output. I build from your real product and iterate until it holds up at full screen — and if a concept can't be made to look right, I'll tell you instead of shipping it.
Do I need to send you my product?
Usually just good photos are enough — multiple angles in decent light. For complex or reflective products, physical samples or a short reference shoot may help, and I'll say so upfront.
Is it legal to use AI ads in my market?
Rules vary by platform and region, and some categories require disclosure. I'll flag what I know for your platform and market, but for anything high-stakes you should confirm with your own legal advisor — I'm not one.
AI ads or UGC ads — which should I run?
Usually both. UGC builds trust because a human is vouching for you; AI gives you volume and speed. They solve different problems, and the brands that win tend to run them together.
