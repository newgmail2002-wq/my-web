Ads that don't look
like ads

The moment someone knows they're watching an ad, their thumb keeps moving. UGC works because it doesn't announce itself — it looks like a person, talking, about something that helped them.

Get a free quote See the work
The honest version

People don't trust brands. They trust people.

Your polished studio spot tells the viewer, in the first frame, that it's an advert — and they've been trained since birth to scroll past it. A creator-style video holding the product at arm's length in a normal room doesn't trip that alarm. It gets watched, and it gets believed.

But UGC isn't just 'film something casual'. The hook has to land in under two seconds, the pacing has to match how people actually scroll, and the ask has to arrive before they leave. That's the craft — and it's the difference between a video that looks authentic and one that performs.

What you get

Included as standard

  • Hooks written to survive the first two seconds
    The opening line is the whole ad. I write and test several per concept, because the difference between hooks is the difference between a winner and wasted spend.
  • Creator-style video that feels real
    Natural delivery, real settings, honest pacing. Made to look like a recommendation, not a commercial.
  • Built for the format it runs in
    Vertical, sound-on, captioned, safe zones respected — so it works natively on Reels, TikTok and Shorts instead of being a landscape ad awkwardly cropped.
  • Multiple variants to test
    Different hooks, angles and CTAs on the same core concept, so you can find the winner with data instead of opinion.
  • Scripts grounded in what you actually sell
    I start from your product, your customer and their real objection — not a trending format that has nothing to do with your business.
  • Raw files and full usage rights
    You own the footage and the cuts. Run them wherever you want, for as long as you want.
Sound familiar?

This is for you if…

  • Your ads look expensive and still don't convert
  • Your cost per acquisition keeps climbing
  • You're launching a product and need social proof before you have any
  • You need volume — fresh creative every week, not one hero video a year

If you recognised yourself in even one of those, it's worth a conversation. It costs nothing to ask.

Questions

UGC Ads FAQ

Do you provide the creator, or do I?
Either. I can produce creator-style content for you, or work with your own creators and handle scripting, direction and editing. Tell me what you've got and we'll pick the cheaper path that still performs.
How many videos do I get?
It depends on the package we scope, but I always recommend multiple variants over a single hero video. Testing three hooks beats perfecting one, nearly every time.
Can you edit footage I've already got?
Yes. If you have raw creator footage or product clips sitting unused, I can cut, hook and caption them into ads that actually run.
Do you run the ads too?
I build the creative. If you want the media buying handled as well, tell me in the enquiry and we'll talk about scope — I'd rather point you to a specialist than pretend.
