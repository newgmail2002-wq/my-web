The honest version

The problem was never the technology

You've seen the demos. You've maybe even paid for a tool that's now sitting unused in a tab. The gap is never the technology — it's that nobody looked at your actual business, found the tasks eating your week, and worked out which of them a machine should be doing.

That's the job. I look at how your business really runs, find the handful of things where AI would genuinely pay for itself, and I build those. Not a strategy deck. Not a workshop. The working thing, plus the training so your team can run it after I'm gone.

What you get

Included as standard

  • An honest audit of where AI actually helps
    I look at what your team does all week and find the repetitive, rule-shaped work worth automating — and I'll tell you plainly which of your ideas aren't worth it.
  • The three highest-value automations, built
    Not thirty ideas in a deck. The handful that pay for themselves, actually shipped and running in your business.
  • Wired into the tools you already have
    Your CRM, inbox, spreadsheets, WhatsApp, calendar. It fits how your team already works instead of demanding they change everything.
  • Your team trained to run it
    Plain-English walkthroughs and documentation. Nobody should need to call me to change a message template.
  • A straight answer on cost and payback
    What it costs to build, what it costs to run monthly, and roughly what it saves. If the numbers don't work, I'll say so before you spend.
  • Someone who'll talk you out of it
    The most valuable thing I do is stop people spending money on AI that won't help them. If your problem is a process problem, no model fixes it — and you'll hear that from me for free.

Questions

AI Expert Consulting FAQ

What counts as 'AI expert' — what's your actual background?
I build and ship AI systems in production: voice agents that handle real customer calls, generated ad creative that runs on real budgets, and automations wired into real businesses. Judge it by the work on my Instagram and by the straightness of the answers you get in the first call, not by a certificate.
Do you just advise, or do you build?
I build. Advice without implementation tends to become a document nobody reads. If you only want a second opinion or a sanity check on a vendor, that's fine too — just say so.
Will AI replace my staff?
In my experience it replaces tasks, not people — the admin nobody enjoys. I'll be straight with you about what changes for your team, because pretending otherwise makes rollout fail anyway.
Is my data safe?
It depends entirely on the tools and the setup, and it's a real question that deserves a real answer. I'll walk you through where your data goes, what's retained, and what the options are — including keeping sensitive data out of third-party models.
What if I don't know what I want yet?
That's the normal starting point, and honestly the best one. Tell me what's frustrating about your week and we'll work backwards from there.
